Websites

LumiKey

LumiKey provides visual feedback while playing the keyboard. It was submitted for the 2014 London Music Hackathon and won the 7digital prize. After a recent update it now utilizes the web MIDI api. Plug in your midi device, mess around with the visualiser config, and see what happens!

imPact Remix!

imPact Remix is a "design your own" drum kit iOS application I built with two friends, Sam Zeckendorf and Nate Harada. imPact began as a project funded by Steinway, quickly became a labor of love, and we are still working to roll out new updates.

Grace Wapner Art Portfolio

In the fall of 2012 I worked with New York artist Grace Bakst Wapner to redesign her website to showcase a new series of artwork. The main task of the redesign was to maximize the size of the artwork while maintaining a clean interface.

Alex Rovello Memorial

In spring 2013, a close friend passed away in a cliff diving accident. In honor of his memory, his family began a community fund to refurbish our local tennis courts. Working with his family, I developed and designed the Fund's website.

Hotmap

This iOS app stemmed from a casual question asked by a housemate, and then turned into a fun weekend project. A way to get a better handle on the google maps API and some crude geolocation work.

Go Civics!

Go Civics is a iOS study guide for the US imigration civics test. A user is able to toggle directly between English and Spanish so that ESL hopefuls can practice with ease.

xenoMod

The xenoMod is a unique musical instrument that is the master of midi modulation. The project consists of an arduino, 16 linear soft pot sensors, two circular soft pots, two USB trackballs sitting ontop of pressure sensors, a pedal, an array of buttons and dials, and some LED lights for pizazz. The box then relays input to a max patch hooked up to Reason.
